How People Pay

Your customers pay the way Americans already pay

No new accounts. No learning curve. They use what's already on their phone.

💳
Credit & Debit Card
"Tap and done — Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover"
The most common way Americans pay for anything. Your customers enter their card once and it's saved for future bookings. Apple Pay and Google Pay work too — one tap on their phone.
⚡
Zelle
"Bank to bank — instant, no fees"
Zelle is built into most American bank apps — Bank of America, Chase, Wells Fargo, and more. Money moves instantly between banks with no fees. Over 100 million people already use it. Your customers don't need to download anything new.
💜
Venmo
"The way younger Americans send money"
Venmo is how millennials and Gen Z pay each other. Over 80 million users in the US. Accepting Venmo on your platform means a huge segment of your market can pay the way they're already comfortable with.
🏦
ACH Bank Transfer
"For bigger jobs — direct from their bank account"
When the job is a few hundred dollars or more, some customers prefer to pay directly from their bank account. ACH transfers are secure, standard in American business, and have the lowest processing fees of any method.
🔒
Escrow — Money Held Safe
"Like real estate escrow — but for any service"
If you've bought a house, you know escrow. The money sits in a neutral account until the deal closes. We do the same for services. Customer pays → money waits → job gets done → customer confirms → money released to the professional. Nobody can run with the cash.
💸
Automatic Payouts
"Professionals get paid — you don't lift a finger"
When a job is approved, the app splits the money automatically. The professional's share goes to their bank account. Your cut goes to your business account. No invoices. No checks. No follow-up. It just happens — same day or next business day.
